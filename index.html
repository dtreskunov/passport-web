Passport Photo

Takes a square US-passport-spec crop from your webcam. Runs entirely in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

Click "Start camera" to begin.

Targets

US spec

  • Square 1:1
  • 600–1200 px per side
  • Head height 50–69%
  • Eye line 56–69% from bottom
  • No digital alteration beyond cropping

Tips

  • Plain light background
  • Face the camera straight on, neutral expression
  • Even lighting on both sides of the face
  • No glasses, no head covering (unless religious)
